This website was designed with two obvious purposes in mind: to give me an opportunity to learn and to give myself a central place to show-case myself to the world .

I'm a Senior at Lawrence University, with a degree in Economics. However, like most liberal arts degrees, the end title doesn't do much justice in summarizing my interests and constantly growing skills in other fields outside of explaining market supply/demand and running cool regressions.

In my classes outside of the Economics department I've been completing courses in Object-Oriented Programming and Data Structures, alongside higher-level Statistics. Outside of the classroom I've been learning web development and hands-on ecological management. For the past three summers, I've been engaged in restoration ecology, learning plants deep to their roots with botanical classifications and interdependent species.

If anything at all, I hope this small little website grows to contain the breadth of my interests in ecological stewardship , coding, and statistical questioning !
